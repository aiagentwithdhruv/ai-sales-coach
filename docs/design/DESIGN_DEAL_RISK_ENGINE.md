# Design Document: Deal Momentum & Risk Engine

**Feature:** Deal Momentum & Risk Engine  
**Priority:** P0 (Phase 2)  
**Designer:** AI Sales Coach Team  
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-01  

---

## 1. Overview

### 1.1 Purpose
Proactively identify deals at risk of stalling or losing by analyzing call patterns, email activity, and conversation content. Provide actionable coaching to get deals back on track.

### 1.2 User Goals
- **Sales Reps**: Get early warning when a deal needs attention
- **Managers**: Prioritize coaching on high-value at-risk deals
- **RevOps**: Improve forecast accuracy with risk-adjusted pipeline

### 1.3 Design Principles
- **Proactive**: Alert before deals go cold, not after
- **Actionable**: Every risk links to a specific action
- **Contextual**: Show evidence, not just scores
- **Non-intrusive**: Don't overwhelm with false alarms

---

## 2. Risk Signal Framework

### 2.1 Risk Categories

| Category | Signals | Severity | Action |
|----------|---------|----------|--------|
| **Engagement Drop** | No contact in 7+ days | Medium | Send follow-up |
| **Champion Gone Dark** | Key contact unresponsive | High | Multi-thread |
| **Vague Next Steps** | "Let's touch base" language | Medium | Lock specific date |
| **Unresolved Objection** | Objection raised, not addressed | High | Provide rebuttal |
| **Competitor Mentioned** | Named competitor in call | Medium | Competitive positioning |
| **Missing Decision Maker** | No exec engagement | High | Ask for intro |
| **Timeline Slip** | Decision date pushed | Medium | Re-qualify urgency |
| **Budget Concerns** | Budget objection unresolved | High | ROI justification |

### 2.2 Health Score Calculation

```
Health Score = 100 - (Risk Penalty Points)

Risk Penalties:
- No contact 7+ days: -15
- Vague next steps: -10
- Unresolved objection: -20
- Missing decision maker: -15
- Competitor mentioned (unaddressed): -10
- Champion gone dark: -25
- Budget concerns: -15

Bonus Points:
- Multi-threaded (3+ contacts): +10
- Demo completed: +10
- Proposal sent: +10
- Verbal commit received: +15
```

## 5. Interaction Patterns

### 5.1 Dismiss Risk Alert

1. User clicks "Dismiss" on risk card
2. Modal appears: "Why are you dismissing this alert?"
   - Already addressed
   - Not relevant to this deal
   - False positive
   - Other (text input)
3. User selects reason
4. Alert removed from view
5. Toast: "Alert dismissed. Undo for 10 seconds."

### 5.2 Quick Actions

**Send Follow-up Email:**
1. Click "Send Follow-up"
2. Opens Follow-up Autopilot with deal context pre-loaded
3. Email pre-drafted based on risk (e.g., re-engagement for no contact)

**Get Competitive Intel:**
1. Click "Get Competitive Intel"
2. Opens modal with battle card for mentioned competitor
3. Shows key differentiators and objection responses

**Draft Intro Request:**
1. Click "Draft Intro Request"
2. Opens email composer with template:
   "Hi [Champion], I'd love to connect with [Missing Stakeholder] to discuss [Topic]. Would you be able to introduce us?"

### 5.3 Deal Table Sorting

- Click column header to sort
- Default: Health score (ascending, worst first)
- Secondary: Value (descending)
- Tertiary: Last contact (ascending, oldest first)
