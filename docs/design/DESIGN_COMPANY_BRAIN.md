# Design Document: Company Brain (Knowledge Base)

**Feature:** Company Brain / Knowledge Training  
**Priority:** P0 (Phase 2)  
**Designer:** AI Sales Coach Team  
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-01  

---

## 1. Overview

### 1.1 Purpose
Enable companies to upload their sales materials (product docs, pricing, FAQs, battle cards) and automatically generate training content, quizzes, and updated talk tracks for sales reps.

### 1.2 User Goals
- **Enablement Team**: Upload new materials, auto-generate training
- **Sales Reps**: Access up-to-date product knowledge, take quizzes
- **Managers**: Track which reps have completed training on new materials

### 1.3 Design Principles
- **Effortless Upload**: Drag-drop, bulk upload, URL import
- **Smart Extraction**: AI identifies key points automatically
- **Always Current**: Version tracking, update notifications
- **Measurable**: Quiz scores, completion rates
