# Design Document: AI Calling Agent (Outbound + Inbound)

**Feature:** AI Calling Agent  
**Priority:** P0 (Phase 3)  
**Designer:** AI Sales Coach Team  
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-01  

---

## 1. Overview

### 1.1 Purpose
Enable companies to scale both **outbound** and **inbound** calling with AI agents that qualify leads, handle objections, answer questions, and book meetings â€” automatically updating CRM with outcomes.

**Outbound**: AI proactively calls lead lists, qualifies prospects, and books meetings.  
**Inbound**: AI answers incoming calls, qualifies callers, routes appropriately, and reduces rep workload.

### 1.2 User Goals

**Outbound:**
- **Sales Ops**: Upload lead lists and launch calling campaigns
- **SDR Managers**: Monitor AI performance and meeting bookings
- **Reps**: Review AI-booked meetings and follow up

**Inbound:**
- **Support/Sales Teams**: Reduce call volume stress and handle overflow
- **Ops Managers**: Ensure every inbound call is answered and qualified
- **Reps**: Receive pre-qualified leads with context, ready to close

### 1.3 Design Principles
- **Transparent**: Always disclose AI identity to callers
- **Controllable**: Easy to pause, adjust scripts, set routing rules
- **Compliant**: Built-in Do Not Call, consent tracking, AI disclosure
- **Measurable**: Clear metrics on calls, connects, qualifications, meetings
- **Always Available**: Inbound AI answers 24/7, no missed calls
